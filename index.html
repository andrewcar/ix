About the Band

IX is an American metalcore band from Brooklyn, New York, formed in late 2015. The band is composed of lead vocalist Bobby Buffaloboy, guitarist and vocalist Jesse Cook, keyboardist Fabio Piparo, bassist Aaron C-T, and drummer Andrew Carvajal.

Their music incorporates harmonic jazz concepts and ferocious breakdowns to illuminate the depths of human suffering and the grace that blossoms from darkness.

The band released their self-recorded debut EP, Into the Depths, in early 2017.
